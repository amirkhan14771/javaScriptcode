About Us

This is an official website of gulamane Ashraf Welfare Society running under guardianship of AALE RASOOL JIGAR GOSHA-E-SHAHEEDE RAHE MADINA MOINUL MASHAIKH HUZOOR SYED MOIN ASHRAF JILANI SAHAB QIBLA. (SAJJADA NASHIN AASTANA PAAK MAKHDOOM ASHRAF JAHANGIR SIMNANI KICHHOCHHA SHARIF).

This is an Official Website Of Gulamane Ashraf Welfare Society Running Under the Supervision of GAWS team. Gulamane Ashraf Welfare Society A Non-governmental organisation (NGO) Working under the patronship of Aale Rasool Maulana Syed Noor Miya Ashrafi Sahab Qibla. This Society Working Since 2016 and Got Registered Under Rajasthan Government Cooperative Department in 2020.This Society organizing blood donation camps, Free Medical Camps, Helping Poor Peoples, and Religious Activities.

Mission

To inspire a believer to remain steadfast in fulfilling the commands of Allah, the Almighty & to shine as a true, humble and obedient devotee of the Beloved Prophet (Peace & Blessings of Allah Be upon Him) by providing educational, spiritual & counselling assistance to the entire community.

Vision

Gulamane Ashraf Welfare Society promote sufism and education. Gulamane Ashraf Welfare Society envisions an environment of love, understanding, peace and harmony on the entire planet, through the propagation of the true beauty of Islam, as taught in the glorious quran & traditions of the holy prophet (peace & blessings of Allah be upon him).

Activity

Gulamane Ashraf Welfare Society on different occasions donates food, blanket, and clothes, to the less fortunate's. To helping poor people. This society organizing blood donation camps, free medical camps and religious activities.
